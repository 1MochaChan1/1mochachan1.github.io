A pixel-art horror adventure where sound is your greatest asset... and your worst enemy. Every noise echoes in the darkness.

## Current Progress

- Core mechanics implemented
- 40% of the story written
- Placeholder art in progress
- Audio design phase upcoming

## Planned Features

- Non-linear exploration with branching storylines
- Advanced audio-based detection system
- Pixel art aesthetics with horror atmosphere
- Multiple endings based on player awareness

## Development

This project combines my passion for atmospheric horror with pixel art created in Aseprite. Development is ongoing, expect updates soon!
